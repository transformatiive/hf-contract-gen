AGREEMENT TO WET LEASE

The Wet Lessor shall make the Aircraft available on Wet Lease to the Wet Lessee and the Wet Lessee shall take the Aircraft on Wet Lease from the Wet Lessor for the duration of the Wet Lease Term subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement and its Flight Program.

The Wet Lessee shall not, at any time during the Wet Lease Term, let, wet lease, hire or otherwise make available the Aircraft to anyone else without the Wet Lessor’s prior written consent.

The obligations set forth in this Agreement will commence and become into full force and effect upon receipt by the Wet Lessor into the Wet Lessor’s Account of the Deposit and the 1st due payment from the Wet Lessee and on receipt of the Deposit and the 1st due payment the Wet Lessor will make the Aircraft available to the Wet Lessee for performance of the agreed Flight Program.

The Aircraft shall be delivered and redelivered at the Wet Lessor’s base. The Wet Lessee shall pay all costs, duly justified, incurred in connection with the positioning of the Aircraft from the Wet Lessor’s base to the Wet Lessee’s base at the commencement of the Wet Lease Term and from the Wet Lessee’s base to the Wet Lessor’s base at the end of Wet Lease Term. The Block Hours flown and Cycles used when positioning the Aircraft in any given period are qualifying Block Hours and Cycles for the purposes of this Agreement.

Any additional costs, incurred by the Wet Lessor as a consequence of operating flights hereunder, to or from a location which is outside the Wet Lessee’s base, at the request of the Wet Lessee, shall be paid by the Wet Lessee in addition to all other amounts payable by the Wet Lessee hereunder.

The Aircraft shall be delivered and redelivered in Lessor’s livery. Should any Aircraft modification (including but not limited to painting) be agreed between the Wet Lessor and the Wet Lessee, the cost thereof shall be borne by the Wet Lessee and shall be payable, on demand, by the Wet Lessee to the Wet Lessor. The Wet Lessee will also pay the Wet Lessor on demand any costs incurred reinstating the Aircraft to its condition prior to these modifications. Any Aircraft modification that may be agreed between the Wet Lessor and the Wet Lessee is subject to approval from the Maltese Civil Aviation Authorities (TMCAD).

At the end of the Wet Lease Term, the return of the Aircraft shall conform to the following conditions (at the cost of the Wet Lessee): interior cleaned by airline standards and full-on board equipment.

The Aircraft shall be delivered without catering equipment. This equipment will be supplied by the Wet Lessee and will be removed prior to redelivery of the Aircraft to the Wet Lessor at the end of the Wet Lease Term. Should the Wet Lessee request the Wet Lessor to provide catering equipment all costs related to providing and position/deposition of the catering equipment will be to the account of the Wet Lessee.

The Aircraft shall be delivered without ULD’s. This equipment will be supplied by the Wet Lessee and will be removed prior to redelivery of the Aircraft to the Wet Lessor at the end of the Wet Lease Term. Should the Wet Lessee request the Wet Lessor to provide ULD’s all costs related to providing and position/deposition the ULD’s will be to the account of the Wet Lessee. Each AKE/LD3 lightweight rental cost is EUR 71 and each pallet rental cost is EUR 41.
