2. AGREEMENT TO WET LEASE

The Wet Lessor shall make the Aircraft available on Wet Lease to the Wet Lessee for the duration of the Wet Lease Term subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement and its Flight Program. Obligations commence upon receipt into the Wet Lessor’s Account of the Deposit and the 1st due payment. The Aircraft is delivered and redelivered at the Wet Lessor’s base in Lessor’s livery. [Full operational, positioning, livery, catering, ULD and equipment-replacement provisions per the executed agreement.]

3. LOSS OF AIRCRAFT

If the Aircraft becomes a Total Loss prior to or during the Wet Lease Term the Wet Lessor shall endeavour to provide a substitute aircraft of similar specification; failing which, performance is cancelled without further liability save for accrued rights and liabilities.

4. PAYMENT (SEE APPENDIX “A”)

Payment is complete only where cleared funds are in the Wet Lessor’s Bank. Late payment carries interest of 1.5% per month. Time of payment is of the essence. [Set-off, cancellation-fee and repudiation provisions per Appendix A.]

5. WET LESSOR’S OBLIGATIONS

The Wet Lessor shall provide, operate, maintain, crew, control and insure the Aircraft in accordance with all applicable laws of the State of Registration; warrants a current certificate of airworthiness; and, subject to availability, may provide a replacement aircraft in AOG situations not caused by the Wet Lessee. [Force-majeure, inspection, spares and IFE provisions per the executed agreement.]

6. WET LESSEE’S OBLIGATIONS

The Wet Lessee shall arrange and pay all Direct Operating Costs; ensure supplier contracts (ground handling of type IATA SGHA 2006 or newer) are provided to the Wet Lessor; provide car, office and spares storage at the Wet Lessee’s Base; book cockpit crew air transport in Business class; and bear EU 261/2004 passenger-compensation costs as operating air carrier. [JFK catering-security, fuel-quality, pushback/towing and high-risk-area provisions per the executed agreement.]

7. LIABILITIES AND INSURANCE

Liability is allocated per clause 7.1. Each party maintains insurance per clause 7.2, including AVN52E extended coverage for a combined single limit of not less than USD 1,000,000,000 any one aircraft/occurrence, with cross-additional-insured and waiver-of-subrogation provisions.

8. TAXATION

The Wet Lessee shall hold the Wet Lessor free and harmless from taxes, duties and fees arising from this Agreement or the operation of the Aircraft, except taxes levied by the Maltese Government.

9. DEFAULT

Breach entitles the innocent party to treat the failure as repudiation. Events of default include non-payment of the Wet Lease Price on the due date and unremedied breaches, insolvency events and enforcement processes, per clauses 9(a)–(e).

11. ASSIGNMENT AND SUB-CONTRACTING

Neither party may assign or pledge its rights without the other’s consent (not to be unreasonably withheld). The Wet Lessor may provide a third-party-operated aircraft of no less payload/configuration, subject to the Wet Lessee’s agreement.

12. MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

This Agreement contains the entire agreement between the parties. Amendments are binding only in writing and signed by both parties. Severability applies. Clause headings are for convenience only.

13. APPLICABLE LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement is governed by the laws of Malta and the Wet Lessee submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Maltese courts or any other EU court chosen by the Wet Lessor. The foregoing does not preclude the Wet Lessor from starting legal action against the Wet Lessee in {{alt_jurisdiction}} if, in its sole opinion, it better protects its interests.
